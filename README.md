# ⚔️ RPG Economy: Bedrock Overhaul

> **<span style="color:#ffd700">Turn your vanilla survival world into a ruthless, data-driven MMORPG ecosystem. Where wealth is tied to a living stock market, power is forged through blood, and death carries a heavy price.</span>**

**RPG Economy** completely abandons the traditional "kill mob, get coin" formula. We have transformed the vanilla Bedrock engine into a massive, heavily calculated RPG module operating entirely within a single <span style="color:#55ffff"><strong>Behavior Pack</strong></span>

No experimental toggles. No messy resource packs. <span style="color:#55ff55"><strong>100% Achievement-friendly</strong></span>

***

## 💠 The "Smart" Interface (Next-Gen UX)
You shouldn't have to navigate clunky menus just to check your power
* **Action Bar Scanner:** Simply hold any market commodity or weapon. The system actively scans the item and displays its real-time market price, 24h trend **(**<span style="color: #00C853;">▲</span>/<span style="color: #D50000;">▼</span>**)**, active enchantments, and calculates its exact **Power** directly on your screen
* **Dynamic Nametags:** Player nametags function as live RPG identifiers, displaying their current Rank Tier, Legacy Level, Combat Power `⚔`, and Echoes `⛃` balance
* **Cinematic Summoning:** Gacha pulls aren't just boring menu clicks. The system temporarily locks your movement, fades the environment, and plays a dynamic frame-by-frame visual sequence on your screen before revealing your loot with tier-specific VFX
* **Immersive Mob Assessment:** Monster nametags feature 12 distinct dark-fantasy RPG tiers from *Feral* to *Zenith*, visual grade indicators `◈`, and directly display the entity's Combat Power `⚔`. Never guess if an enemy is out of your league again

## 📈 A Living, Breathing Market
The economy is centralized around Echoes and reacts entirely to global player activity
* **Supply, Demand & Volatility:** Purchasing an asset actively drives its global price up (Ask), while mass-selling crashes it (Bid). Prices naturally fluctuate daily based on programmed asset volatility and 24-hour trading volumes
* **Live Order Book:** Every transaction includes a dynamic spread tax, forcing you to trade strategically
* **Pawn Shop Appraisal:** Unwanted gear? Sell it directly from your inventory. The system appraises the exact value of your equipment based on item type, remaining durability, enchantment levels, and custom lore stats

## 🩸 Ruthless Progression & Combat
Your power is no longer determined by vanilla armor. It is determined by your <span style="color:#ffaa00"><strong>Attributes</strong></span> and your <span style="color:#ffaa00"><strong>Rank</strong></span>
* **Attribute Allocation:** Level up your Legacy to earn Stat Points. Manually invest them into ATK, DEF, MAX HP, CRIT RATE, and CRIT DMG via the Atlas UI
* **Dynamic Mob Scaling:** Spawning monsters passively scan the Combat Power (CP) of nearby players. Higher player CP increases the probability of mobs spawning with devastating HP and ATK multiplier buffs
* **PvP Assassinations:** Defeat bosses or hunt other players to steal their Rank Points (RP). The stolen percentage dynamically scales based on the rank gap between the killer and the victim
* **Hardcore Death Penalties:** Engaging in combat triggers a 15-second Combat Tag. **Dying to PvP drops 50% of your total Echoes to the killer.** Dying to PvE permanently burns 20% of your wealth. Survival is mandatory

## ⚒️ Relics & The Forge
* **Banner Summoning:** Test your luck using specific keys to pull from Gacha Banners. Features a hard Pity system guaranteeing <span style="color:#ffd700"><strong>SSR/UR drops</strong></span>, and a Scrap Exchange for duplicate pulls
* **Advanced Enchanting:** Interacting with an Enchantment Table opens a custom UI and strictly requires at least 15 nearby bookshelves. Costs scale dynamically based on the item’s base value, current durability, and conflicting enchantments
* **Gear Reforging & Ascension:** Sacrifice valuable materials at the Smithing Table to mutate your gear's stats via RNG (e.g. transforming ATK into CRIT RATE). Ascend max-level gear to unlock permanent passive bonuses

## 🧭 World Mechanics
* **Lodestone Network:** Anchor your coordinates using Lodestones. Fast travel teleportation costs scale dynamically with the physical block distance between nodes. If an anchored Lodestone is broken, the waypoint seal is permanently destroyed
* **Bounty Board:** Interact with Lecterns to generate 5 daily randomized missions scaled perfectly to your Legacy Tier. Abandoning a job incurs a heavy fine
* **Daily Streaks:** Consecutive logins build streaks for milestone rewards. Missing a single day resets your streak to zero

## ⚙️ Technical Specifications & Installation
* **Format:** <span style="color:#5ff"><strong>Behavior Pack Only</strong></span>
* **Supported Engine:** `stable API 1.19.0` / `UI 1.2.0`
* **Tested Build:** Minecraft Bedrock <span style="color:#5f5"><strong>1.26.0+</strong></span>
* **Compatibility:** Fully supports Realms, Dedicated Servers, and Singleplayer

> <span style="color:#5f5"><strong>Data Integrity:</strong></span> Secured via composite storage, checksum validation, and inventory snapshotting to prevent duping, market manipulation, and combat-logging exploits
